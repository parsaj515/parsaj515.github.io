üêù What ARE Garbage Bees?

Often mistaken for their honey-producing cousins, garbage bees are the unsung heroes of the insect world. While honeybees get all the glory making sweet golden nectar, garbage bees perform the crucial (and frankly, disgusting) work of processing organic waste, composting nature's leftovers, and keeping ecosystems from drowning in decomposing matter.
